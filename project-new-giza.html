New Giza

Luxury Living West of Cairo

Project Overview

Strategically positioned for optimal accessibility, NEWGIZA stands as the first and largest integrated upscale development in Egypt. This expansive project, spanning over 1,500 acres, is located in the 6th of October district, approximately 23 kilometers from Cairo, and enjoys a prime location on the Cairo-Alexandria Road. The future of New Giza includes ten walkable neighborhoods, where lush greenery will be a defining feature, with almost 90% of the development's land allocated to open spaces and wide streets. Residents will enjoy a diverse range of amenities, including a five-star hotel, contemporary offices, diverse restaurants, entertainment venues, sports club, and golf course.
