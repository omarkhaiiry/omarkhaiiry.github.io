IVY New Zayed

A Modern Compound by The G Developments

Project Overview

Welcome to IVY Compound, an exclusive residential development located in the vibrant and fast-growing area of New Zayed, Giza. Developed by The G Developments, IVY offers a blend of elegance, tranquility, and convenience, setting a new benchmark for luxury living in West Cairo.

IVY offers a variety of unit types designed to suit different family sizes and lifestyles. Each unit features contemporary finishes, open living spaces, and serene views.
