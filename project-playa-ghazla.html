Playa Ghazla

Curated Beachfront Homes - New Launch

Project Overview

Discover Playa Ghazala North Coast, a high-end coastal destination masterfully developed by G Developments. Nestled along the longest beach in North Coast, Egypt, stretching across an impressive 1.5 km, Playa Ghazala offers an unparalleled luxury resort experience. This exclusive North Coast resort features an exquisite array of beachfront properties, each boasting elegant Mediterranean-inspired design. As a pet-friendly resort, it ensures the whole family can enjoy this coastal paradise.

Key Features

  • 1.5 km Pristine Beachfront
  • Central Spine (Parks & Greenery)
  • Seaside Promenade
  • Cable Park (Water Sports)
  • Playa Sports Club (Soccer, Tennis, Squash)
  • LA7 Beach Facility
  • Fine Dining & Retail
